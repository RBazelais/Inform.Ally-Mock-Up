Faculty

As a professor, cancelling or modifying a class is a simple point and click process, where generic messages can be sent out to students through several mediums in seconds.

Students

Students will be able receive or send a message to their professor if they will not be able to make it to class with an explanation.

The Inform.ally calendar keeps track of every course and time. Messages can be sent through various means on the laptops or mobile devices, greatly increasing the likelihood that a crucial message can make it to them in time to wasting time and energy.
